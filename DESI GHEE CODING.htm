About Us

We are a family-owned business in Pakistan, dedicated to preserving the age-old art of making desi ghee. Sourced from local organic farms, our ghee is made using the traditional bilona method – slow-churned for maximum purity and nutrition. Rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K, it's perfect for cooking biryanis, parathas, or even as a health tonic.

  • 100% Organic & Chemical-Free
  • Halal Certified
  • Grass-Fed Cows
  • Supports Local Farmers

Why choose us? In a world of processed fats, our desi ghee brings back the authentic taste of home – just like your nani's kitchen!
